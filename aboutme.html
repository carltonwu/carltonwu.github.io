About Me

I am a currently a student studying Computer Science at Rutgers University New Brunswick. My passion for space and computer science merged at the Rutgers Rocket Propulsion Laboratory. Currently I am telemetry Team Lead managing and overseeing the development of embedded software, graphical interfaces, and RF circuits. At Spaceport America, we placed 2nd as a team in design and were the only successful two-stage rocket out of over 140 teams for 2022.


I am also the developer behind the AIAA website for Rutgers. Previously I have interned as a web developer for the department of Student Affairs at Rutgers University New Brunswick.
